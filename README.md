# Dominos
The points of the game is to empty all the pieces in your hand , each piece has two
numbers and to place a piece on the board the numbers must match.

## Running the game

There will be a ready application included in the files to try and run the game,
host your own game and tell your friend to click the join button and enter the
hosterâ€™s ip address and enjoy!.

## How to play
Each player has 7 pieces, you can get extra pieces if needed from the draw pile on the bottom right corner.
To play a piece you have to click on it , a screen will pop up asking you where you want to play it if you decide to put it on the left or on the right the piece will be rotated -90 degrees and will be played if you decide to flip the piece it will be rotated the opposite way, if your move is illegal you are asked to play again.
